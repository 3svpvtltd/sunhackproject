Frequently Asked Questions

Is EduZen suitable for my child's age?

EduZen is specifically designed for children aged 6-12 years, with content tailored to different grade levels and learning abilities.

How do I track my child's progress?

Parents can access detailed progress reports through the profile section, showing completed lessons, earned badges, and learning achievements.

Is the platform safe for children?

Yes! EduZen prioritizes child safety with secure content, no external links, and a monitored learning environment.

Can my child use EduZen on mobile devices?

Absolutely! EduZen is fully responsive and works seamlessly on tablets, smartphones, and desktop computers.
